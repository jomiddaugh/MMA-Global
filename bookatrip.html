Destination

Thailand & Myan Mar

Our Thailand and Myan Mar trip will take you back to the origins of the art of 8 limbs.

North America Tour

The North America allows you to train and get instruction from some of MMA's most admired coaches and competitors. You will start in Sacramento, training with Uriah Faber and his team at Alpha Male. The next stop will be in Portland, where you will get wrestling instruction at Peninsula Wrestling Club. Before heading to the east coast, our tour will take you to Montreal to get instruction from Firas Zihabi and Georges St-Pierre. You will finish the trip training at Sanford MMA getting some of the best kick boxing instruction in the world from Henri Hooft.

Latin America Tour

The start of our Latin America tour starts in Brazil for one reason and one reason only. If it wasn't for Royce Gracie and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, MMA would not be as popular today as it is. Starting in Rio De Janeiro, you will spend 6 days triaing at Alliance Jiu-Jitsu. Next you wll travel across town to spend another 6 days training at Gracie Barra. One of the largest Jiu-Jitsu teams/ franchises in the world. Lastly, you will fly up to Central America to train with our friends south of the border at Lobo Gym. Lobo gym offers high level striking training from one of Mexico's highest regarded gyms.

Greater Europe Tour

If you are a fan of the modern era of MMA you most likely know of two famous fighters, Conor McGregor and Khabib Nurmagomedov. In our Greater Europe tour you can train from the same gyms that helped shape both of these great competitors. You will begin your journey at SBG Ireland in Dublin getting instruction from John Kavanagh. Next you will take a quick hop and a skip across the Irish Sea to train at Team Kaobon in Liverpool. You will get insruction with MMA striking with a primary base in Thai boxing. Kaobon is responsible for some of the UFC's most exciting english fighters Darren Till and Tom Aspinall. Before your trip is over, you will fly down to Dubai to train at TK MMA & Fitness. TK MMA has one of the finest facillities to train mixed martial arts in the Middle East. It is one of the only places where you can get world class Sambo Wrestling instruction. Made famous by The Eagle, Khabib Nurmagomedov.
